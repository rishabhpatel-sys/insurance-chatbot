Sample Policy: HealthCare Basic

Overview:
HealthCare Basic provides hospitalization coverage up to INR 500,000 per year. It covers inpatient treatment, surgical procedures, and some outpatient procedures when pre-authorized.

Key Benefits:

- Inpatient hospitalization
- Daycare procedures
- Pre- and post-hospitalization expenses (30/60 days)
- Room rent limit: ICU unrestricted, normal room up to INR 5,000/day

Claim Process:

1. Intimation: Inform insurer within 24-48 hours of hospitalization.
2. Documents: Policy copy, hospital bills, discharge summary, prescription, ID proof.
3. Settlement: Cashless (network hospital) or reimbursement (non-network). Typical settlement time: 7-15 business days after receiving all documents.

Offers:

- 10% discount on premium for first-time customers for the first year.
