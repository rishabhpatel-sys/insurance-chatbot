Sample Policy: AutoSecure Basic

Overview:
AutoSecure Basic offers third-party liability coverage and optional comprehensive cover for damages up to INR 300,000.

Key Benefits:
- Third-party liability
- Optional own-damage coverage
- Roadside assistance add-on available

Claim Process:
1. Intimation: Call helpline within 48 hours of incident.
2. Survey: Insurer sends a surveyor for damage assessment.
3. Documents: FIR (if applicable), driving license, RC, estimate from workshop.
4. Settlement: Typically 10-20 business days depending on parts availability and survey completion.

Offers:
- Free roadside assistance for first 90 days.
